Pick a legend each round, build all eight slots, and see how far your dream roster runs.

How it works

  • Build 8 slots: QB, RB, three WRs, TE, a whole offensive line, and a whole defense.
  • Every round you get four cards. Each is a franchise, an era, and that team's best legend from it. Pick one.
  • The line and defense slots draft famous units like the Hogs, the Steel Curtain, or the Legion of Boom.
  • Don't love your options? Hit Respin All to reroll all four cards into brand new franchises, eras, and legends. You get four respins per game.
  • Your roster gets one blended rating run through a win curve. Only a near perfect build goes 17 and 0.
