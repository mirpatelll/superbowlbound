# Superbowl Bound

An all-time NFL roster builder game. Pick a legend each round, fill all eight roster
slots (QB, RB, three WRs, TE, a full offensive line, and a full defense), and see how
far your dream roster runs through a simulated season.

## How it works

- Build 8 slots: QB, RB, three WRs, TE, a whole offensive line, and a whole defense.
- Every round you get two cards. Each is a franchise, an era, and that team's best
  legend from it. Pick one.
- The line and defense slots draft famous units — the Hogs, the Steel Curtain, the
  Legion of Boom, and more.
- Use the respin buttons to reroll just a franchise or just an era on either card.
  You get two team respins and two era respins per game.
- Your roster gets one blended rating run through a win curve. Only a near-perfect
  build goes 17-0.

## Modes

- **Classic** — ratings shown. Draft on the numbers.
- **Legend** — ratings hidden. Draft on memory.

## Running it

It's a single self-contained file. Just open `index.html` in any browser, or visit the
deployed site. Built mobile-first, so it plays great on a phone.
